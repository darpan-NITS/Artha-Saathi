# Artha-Saathi — Impact Model

## Problem Size

| Metric | Value | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Demat accounts in India | 14 crore+ | SEBI 2025 |
| Indians without a financial plan | ~95% | ET Markets survey |
| Cost of a human financial advisor | ₹25,000+/year | Industry average |
| Users served by advisors | <2% of investors | SEBI data |

## What Artha-Saathi Replaces

A traditional financial planning engagement involves:
- 2–3 advisor meetings (3–4 hours total)
- Manual tax calculation and form review
- Separate retirement planning consultation
- Annual portfolio review

**Total time: 4–6 hours. Total cost: ₹25,000+/year.**

Artha-Saathi delivers equivalent output in **under 7 minutes, at zero cost.**

## Time Saved
```
Traditional process : 4–6 hours
Artha-Saathi        : 7 minutes
Reduction           : ~97%
```

## TAM Calculation
```
Addressable users    = 14 crore demat accounts
× % without advisor  = 98%
= 13.72 crore users

× Value delivered    = ₹20,000/user/year (conservative)
= ₹2.74 lakh crore TAM
```

## Tax Savings Impact

Average user using the Tax Wizard:
- Unused 80C gap: ~₹80,000 → tax saving ~₹24,000
- Unused 80D gap: ~₹15,000 → tax saving ~₹3,000
- NPS 80CCD(1B): ~₹50,000 → tax saving ~₹15,000
- **Total average tax saving per user: ~₹42,000/year**

At 1 lakh users: **₹420 crore in aggregate tax savings.**

## Assumptions

1. Average Indian salaried professional in 30% tax bracket
2. Conservative CAGR of 12% used for equity projections
3. Indian inflation rate of 6% used for FIRE calculations
4. SWR of 3.5% used (more conservative than US 4% rule due to higher inflation)
5. Financial advisor cost based on Mumbai/Delhi market rates
